<p><strong>The Aircraft Tyres Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Radial</li><li>Bias</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft tyre market is primarily divided into radial and bias tyre types. Radial tyres feature a design where the cords run at a 90-degree angle to the tread, offering superior strength, better heat dissipation, and enhanced fuel efficiency. In contrast, bias tyres have cords arranged at varying angles, providing more stability but less performance in terms of heat and fuel consumption. Each type caters to specific aircraft needs, influencing factors like cost, performance, and operational requirements.</p></p>
