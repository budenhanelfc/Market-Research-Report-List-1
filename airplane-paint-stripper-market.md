<p><p>The airplane paint stripper market comprises three main types: the caustic type, the acidic type, and the solvent type. </p><p>The caustic type utilizes alkaline substances to break down paint, making it effective on tough coatings but potentially damaging to aircraft materials. </p><p>The acidic type employs acids to dissolve paint layers, providing a strong approach but requiring careful handling due to potential corrosion risks. </p><p>The solvent type relies on organic solvents to soften and dissolve paints, offering a milder solution suitable for various surfaces.</p></p>
