<p><strong>The Ammonia Absorption Tower Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Plate Tower</li><li>Spray Tower</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ammonia absorption towers are essential in gas treatment processes, primarily for absorbing ammonia from industrial emissions. </p><p>Plate towers use horizontal plates to enhance contact between gas and liquid, promoting efficient absorption and separation. They are known for their high surface area and easy maintenance.</p><p>Spray towers, conversely, utilize high-pressure nozzles to atomize the liquid, creating a fine mist that maximizes gas-liquid interaction. This type is often preferred for its simplicity and effectiveness in handling varying flow rates. Each type serves specific operational needs in ammonia recovery applications.</p></p>
