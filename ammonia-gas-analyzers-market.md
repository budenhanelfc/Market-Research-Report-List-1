<p><strong>The Ammonia Gas Analyzers Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Colorimetric</li><li>Ion-selective Electrodes (ISE)</li><li>Ultraviolet (UV) Absorbance</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ammonia gas analyzers are categorized into three primary types based on their measurement techniques. Colorimetric analyzers utilize chemical reactions that produce a color change proportional to ammonia concentration, offering straightforward and cost-effective solutions. Ion-selective electrodes (ISE) measure the voltage difference generated by ammonia ions in a sample, providing high specificity and sensitivity. Ultraviolet (UV) absorbance analyzers detect ammonia by measuring the absorption of UV light at specific wavelengths, offering rapid and precise detection often used in various industrial processes.</p></p>
