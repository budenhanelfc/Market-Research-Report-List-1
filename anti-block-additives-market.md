<p><p>Anti-block additives are categorized into two main types: inorganic and organic. Inorganic anti-block additives, such as silica and calcium carbonate, are used to enhance film properties by reducing static cling between layers, improving clarity and processing efficiency. Organic anti-block additives, derived from natural or synthetic sources, often include polymers or waxes that provide similar benefits while also offering flexibility in formulation. Both types are essential for optimizing the performance of packaging materials and films in various applications.</p></p>
