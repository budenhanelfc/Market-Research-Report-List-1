<p><ul><li>Inner-Rotor Motor</li><li>External-Rotor Motor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automobile wheel hub motor market features two main types: inner-rotor and external-rotor motors. Inner-rotor motors have the rotor located within the stator, offering compact designs and efficient torque generation, making them suitable for various vehicle types. In contrast, external-rotor motors position the rotor outside the stator, allowing for larger diameter designs, enhancing cooling and power density. Each motor type presents unique advantages, impacting their application in electric and hybrid vehicles for improved performance and energy efficiency.</p></p>
