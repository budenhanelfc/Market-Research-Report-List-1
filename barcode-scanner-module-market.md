<p><p>The barcode scanner module market is divided into two primary types: 1D and 2D barcode scanners. 1D barcode scanners read linear barcodes, typically used in retail and inventory management, offering quick scanning for basic data retrieval. In contrast, 2D barcode scanners can decode matrix codes like QR codes, which store more information in a compact format, making them ideal for diverse applications such as mobile payments and ticketing. Both types cater to varying industry needs, enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
