<p><p>The breathing equipment market comprises various types, primarily including Self-Contained Breathing Apparatus (SCBA) and Emergency Escape Breathing Apparatus (EEBA). SCBA is designed for use in hazardous environments, providing users with a supply of breathable air from a tank, ensuring safety during firefighting, industrial tasks, or rescue operations. EEBA, on the other hand, is intended for emergency situations, enabling individuals to evacuate safely from areas with toxic gases or smoke. Both types are crucial for ensuring respiratory safety in critical conditions.</p></p>
