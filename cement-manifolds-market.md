<p><ul><li>Simple Plug</li><li>Double Plug</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The cement manifolds market primarily includes two types: simple plug and double plug manifolds. Simple plug manifolds facilitate straightforward cementing operations by allowing a single line for cement flow, ideal for basic applications. In contrast, double plug manifolds feature two plugs, enabling dual cementing operations and providing added safety and control during the process. This distinction caters to varying operational needs in the oil and gas industry, ensuring efficient and secure cementing in well construction and maintenance.</p></p>
