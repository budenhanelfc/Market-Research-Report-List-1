<p><p>The decanter centrifuge market is primarily segmented into two types: three-phase and two-phase decanter centrifuges. A three-phase decanter centrifuge efficiently separates solid, liquid, and a second liquid phase, making it ideal for applications like oil and wastewater treatment. In contrast, a two-phase decanter centrifuge separates solids from a single liquid phase, commonly used in processes such as food and beverage production. Each type serves specific industrial needs, enhancing separation efficiency and product quality in various sectors.</p></p>
