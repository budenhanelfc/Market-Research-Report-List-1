<p><p>Fibre Reinforced Plastic (FRP) is a composite material composed of a polymer matrix reinforced with fibres, enhancing strength and durability. The FRP market is primarily categorized into two types: PAN-based and pitch-based. PAN-based FRP uses polyacrylonitrile as the precursor, resulting in high tensile strength and thermal stability, making it suitable for aerospace and automotive applications. In contrast, pitch-based FRP, derived from petroleum or natural gas, offers superior thermal conductivity and is commonly used in applications like aerospace and construction.</p></p>
