<p><ul><li>AFIS Technology</li><li>Non-AFIS Technology</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The fingerprint biometrics market is divided into two primary types: AFIS (Automated Fingerprint Identification System) technology and non-AFIS technology. AFIS technology enables the automated capture and comparison of fingerprint data against extensive databases for identification purposes, commonly used in law enforcement and security applications. Non-AFIS technology, on the other hand, involves basic fingerprint recognition solutions that do not rely on centralized databases, often utilized for access control, time tracking, and personal device security, catering to a more diverse range of applications.</p></p>
