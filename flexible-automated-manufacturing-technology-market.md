<p><p>The Flexible Automated Manufacturing Technology market encompasses three primary types: Flexible Manufacturing Cells (FMC), which are compact, adaptable systems designed to produce a variety of parts with minimal setup time; Flexible Manufacturing Systems (FMS), which integrate multiple machines and automated processes to handle varying product requirements efficiently; and Flexible Automatic Production Lines, which incorporate advanced automation and robotics to streamline large-scale production while maintaining the ability to switch between different products quickly. Each type enhances efficiency and responsiveness in manufacturing.</p></p>
