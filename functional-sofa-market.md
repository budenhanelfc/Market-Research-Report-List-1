<p><p>The Functional Sofa market can be categorized into three main types: Manual Function Sofas, Electric Function Sofas, and Other market segments. Manual Function Sofas are designed with mechanical mechanisms allowing users to adjust the positions manually, providing flexibility without the need for power. Electric Function Sofas, on the other hand, utilize motorized systems for effortless adjustments at the touch of a button, enhancing convenience and comfort. The "Other" category includes specialized or hybrid models that may combine features from both types or incorporate additional functionalities.</p></p>
