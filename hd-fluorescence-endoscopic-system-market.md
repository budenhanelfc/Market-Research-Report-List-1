<p><p>The HD Fluorescence Endoscopic System market can be categorized into two main types: homochromy and color. Homochromy utilizes a single wavelength for imaging, enhancing contrast and visibility of tissues during procedures, making it effective for identifying lesions. In contrast, the color market leverages multiple wavelengths to provide a broader spectrum of visualization, allowing for more detailed anatomical differentiation. Both types enhance the precision and efficacy of endoscopic procedures, improving diagnostic accuracy and therapeutic outcomes in clinical settings.</p></p>
