<p><strong>The Heart Catheterization Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Angiography Catheters</li><li>Guiding Catheters</li><li>Pulmonary Artery Catheters</li><li>PTCA Balloon Catheters</li><li>Intravascular Ultrasound (IVUS) Catheters</li><li>Electrophysiology Catheters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heart catheterization market comprises various catheter types used for diagnostic and therapeutic interventions in cardiovascular procedures. Angiography catheters are employed to visualize blood vessels, while guiding catheters facilitate the placement of other devices. Pulmonary artery catheters monitor heart function and pressures. PTCA balloon catheters are used for angioplasty to open blocked arteries. Intravascular ultrasound (IVUS) catheters provide real-time imaging of vascular structures, and electrophysiology catheters assess electrical activity within the heart for arrhythmia management.</p></p>
