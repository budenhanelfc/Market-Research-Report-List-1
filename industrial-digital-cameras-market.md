<p><ul><li>CMOS</li><li>CCD</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The industrial digital cameras market primarily features two sensor types: CMOS (Complementary Metal-Oxide-Semiconductor) and CCD (Charge-Coupled Device). CMOS cameras are known for their lower power consumption and faster processing speeds, making them suitable for high-speed applications. In contrast, CCD cameras excel in image quality and lower noise levels, often preferred for critical imaging tasks. Both technologies cater to various applications, including manufacturing, quality control, and machine vision, highlighting the diverse needs within the industrial sector.</p></p>
