<p><p>The industrial pallet conveyor market encompasses various types designed for efficient material handling. Drag chain conveyors utilize linked chains to move pallets, ideal for heavy loads. Roller conveyors use cylindrical rollers for smooth transport, suitable for diverse pallet sizes. Plastic type conveyors incorporate durable plastic components, offering resistance to chemicals and corrosion. Slat type conveyors feature slats for stable pallet support, ensuring reliability in movement. Other types may include belt-driven or modular systems, providing tailored solutions for specific industrial needs.</p></p>
