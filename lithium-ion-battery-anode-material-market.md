<p><p>The lithium-ion battery anode material market includes several key types, each with distinct characteristics. LiMn2O4 (lithium manganese oxide) provides better thermal stability and safety, often utilized in electric vehicles. LiNiO2 (lithium nickel oxide) features high capacity and energy density. LiFePO4 (lithium iron phosphate) is known for its stability and long cycle life, making it ideal for power tools and electric applications. Other materials encompass emerging alternatives and combinations.</p></p>
