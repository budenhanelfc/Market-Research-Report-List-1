<p><strong>The Low-E Glass Coating Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Sputtered (Soft Coating)</li><li>Pyrolytic (Hard Coating)</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Low-E Glass Coating market is primarily divided into two types: Sputtered (Soft Coating) and Pyrolytic (Hard Coating). Sputtered coatings are applied through vacuum deposition, offering superior performance in energy efficiency and visual clarity, making them ideal for high-performance windows. In contrast, pyrolytic coatings are applied during the manufacturing process of glass, resulting in a durable, heat-treated finish that withstands harsher environments. Each type serves distinct applications, catering to varying aesthetic and functional requirements in the construction industry.</p></p>
