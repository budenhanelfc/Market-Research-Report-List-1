<p><p>The lubricant for hot forging market consists of two primary types: graphite-containing lubricants and graphite-free lubricants. Graphite-containing lubricants enhance the lubrication process with solid particles that reduce friction and improve tool life. They are effective in high-temperature conditions but may leave residues. In contrast, graphite-free lubricants utilize alternative materials to provide a clean application, reducing post-processing cleanup while maintaining effective lubrication. Both types aim to improve forging efficiency and extend tool durability, catering to different industry needs.</p></p>
