<p><strong>The Marine 4 Stroke Engine Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Heavy Fuel Oil</li><li>Marine Diesel Oil</li><li>Marine Gas Oil</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Marine 4 Stroke Engine market comprises three key fuel types: Heavy Fuel Oil (HFO), Marine Diesel Oil (MDO), and Marine Gas Oil (MGO). HFO, a viscous and cost-effective option, is popular in large vessels but requires preheating. MDO, a blend of HFO and diesel, offers better combustion and is suitable for medium-speed engines. MGO, a lighter, low-sulfur fuel, is essential for compliance with stringent emissions regulations and is favored for its cleaner burning properties, especially in coastal and sensitive areas.</p></p>
