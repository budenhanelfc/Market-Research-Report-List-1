<p><strong>The Medical Incision Film Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>PU Film</li><li>PE Film</li><li>Iodophor Film</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The medical incision film market encompasses various types of protective films used to cover surgical incisions. PU (Polyurethane) film is breathable, flexible, and waterproof, promoting healing while protecting the wound. PE (Polyethylene) film offers a cost-effective barrier against contaminants but lacks breathability. Iodophor film is impregnated with iodine, providing antimicrobial properties that help prevent infection at the incision site. Each film type serves distinct applications within medical settings, focusing on wound care, infection prevention, and promoting optimal recovery environments.</p></p>
