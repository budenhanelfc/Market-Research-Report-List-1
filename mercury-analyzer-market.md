<p><strong>The Mercury Analyzer Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption Mercury Analyzer</li><li>Cold Atomic Fluorescence Mercury Analyzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Mercury Analyzer market is primarily categorized into two types: Cold Vapor Atomic Absorption (CVAA) Mercury Analyzers and Cold Atomic Fluorescence (CAF) Mercury Analyzers. CVAA analyzers utilize atomic absorption spectroscopy to measure mercury concentrations by vaporizing samples in a cold environment, offering high sensitivity and reliability. In contrast, CAF analyzers employ fluorescence techniques to detect mercury at very low levels, ensuring better selectivity and precision. Both types are crucial for environmental monitoring, industrial quality control, and health safety assessments.</p></p>
