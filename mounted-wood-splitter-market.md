<p><p>The mounted wood splitter market primarily includes electric and hydraulic wood splitters. Electric wood splitters are powered by electricity, making them suitable for residential use with lower output but quieter operation. In contrast, hydraulic wood splitters utilize hydraulic pressure to deliver powerful splitting force, ideal for heavy-duty tasks and larger logs. While electric models prioritize convenience and portability, hydraulic variants emphasize efficiency and strength, making them well-suited for commercial applications and extensive wood processing.</p></p>
