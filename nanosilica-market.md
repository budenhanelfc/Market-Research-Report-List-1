<p><strong>The NanoSilica Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>P type</li><li>S type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The NanoSilica market can be categorized into two main types: P-type and S-type. P-type NanoSilica typically refers to particulate forms, which are used primarily in industrial applications, providing enhanced strength and durability in materials like concrete and plastics. In contrast, S-type NanoSilica is characterized by its ultra-fine, spherical particles, often utilized in high-performance coatings, electronics, and cosmetics for its superior dispersion and surface area. Both types cater to distinct sectors, driving innovation and functionality in their respective applications.</p></p>
