<p><p>NIR sorting systems utilize near-infrared spectroscopy to identify and separate materials based on their chemical composition. The market comprises two primary types: crawler sorters and vertical sorters. Crawler sorters are designed for continuous material flow, efficiently handling large volumes while maintaining precision. In contrast, vertical sorters optimize space and enhance sorting accuracy by positioning sensors directly above the material, improving detection capabilities. Both types are crucial for industries like recycling, agriculture, and plastics, enhancing efficiency and sustainability.</p></p>
