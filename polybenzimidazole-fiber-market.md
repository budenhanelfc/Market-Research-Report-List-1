<p><p>The Polybenzimidazole (PBI) fiber market is primarily divided into two types: PBI filament and PBI staple fiber. PBI filament is characterized by continuous strands of fiber, offering high strength and thermal stability, making it suitable for applications in industrial textiles and protective clothing. PBI staple fiber, on the other hand, consists of shorter fibers that can be spun into yarns, providing versatility for various textile products. Both types are valued for their exceptional heat resistance and flame-retardant properties.</p></p>
