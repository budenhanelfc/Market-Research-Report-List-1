<p><p>The portable screw compressor market is primarily divided into two types: oil-injected and oil-free portable screw compressors. Oil-injected portable screw compressors utilize oil for lubrication and cooling, enhancing efficiency and output, making them suitable for heavy-duty applications. In contrast, oil-free portable screw compressors operate without oil, delivering clean, contamination-free air, ideal for sensitive industries like pharmaceuticals and food processing. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, balancing performance, maintenance, and air purity based on specific operational requirements.</p></p>
