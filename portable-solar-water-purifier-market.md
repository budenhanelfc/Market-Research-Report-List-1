<p><p>The portable solar water purifier market features various filtration systems, notably the Ultra-Filtration System and Reverse Osmosis (RO) Filtration System, along with other technologies. The Ultra-Filtration System uses membrane filtration to remove large particles, bacteria, and viruses, making it ideal for surface water sources. In contrast, the RO Filtration System effectively eliminates a broader range of contaminants, including dissolved solids, ensuring high water purity. The "Others" category encompasses additional methods such as UV purification and activated carbon filters, catering to diverse consumer needs.</p></p>
