<p><p>The professional bicycle helmets market typically features two main types: half shell helmets and full face helmets. Half shell helmets offer lightweight protection and are well-ventilated, making them ideal for road cycling and general use. They cover the top and back of the head but leave the face exposed. In contrast, full face helmets provide comprehensive protection, including the face and chin, making them suitable for downhill and extreme cycling, where the risk of impacts is higher. Each type caters to specific cycling needs and safety requirements.</p></p>
