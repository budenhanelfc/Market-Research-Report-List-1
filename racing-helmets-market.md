<p><p>The racing helmets market consists of three main types: full face, open face, and half helmets. Full face helmets provide comprehensive protection, covering the entire head and face, making them ideal for high-speed racing. Open face helmets offer protection to the head while leaving the face exposed, providing better visibility and comfort, often preferred in less intense settings. Half helmets, also known as shorty helmets, cover only the top of the head, offering minimal protection and lightweight design, suitable for casual riding.</p></p>
