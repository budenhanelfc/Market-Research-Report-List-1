<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Reducing Flanges Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Weld-Neck Reducing Flanges</li><li>Slip-On Reducing Flanges</li><li>Threaded Reducing Flanges</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The reducing flanges market includes various types tailored for different applications. Weld-neck reducing flanges feature a welded neck, providing strength and a smooth transition for reduced diameters, ideal for high-pressure systems. Slip-on reducing flanges allow easy alignment and are designed to slip over the pipe ends, suitable for lower-pressure applications. Threaded reducing flanges have internal threads for direct connection to pipes, offering quick installation without welding. Each type serves distinct requirements in industrial piping systems.</p></p>
