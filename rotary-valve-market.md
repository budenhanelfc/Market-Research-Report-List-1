<p><ul><li>Butterfly Valve</li><li>Ball Valves</li><li>Plug Valves</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rotary valve market encompasses various types such as butterfly valves, ball valves, and plug valves, each serving unique functions in fluid control. Butterfly valves feature a rotating disc to regulate flow, making them suitable for large volumes. Ball valves, known for their quick on-off capabilities, use a spherical closure element, ideal for high-pressure applications. Plug valves contain a cylindrical plug that rotates to stop or allow flow, offering reliable sealing and ease of maintenance. Each type caters to distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
