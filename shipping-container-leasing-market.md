<p><ul><li>Dry Containers</li><li>Reefer Containers</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The shipping container leasing market primarily comprises two types: dry containers and reefer containers. Dry containers are standard, cargo-carrying units designed for transporting non-perishable goods such as machinery, textiles, and electronics. In contrast, reefer containers are specialized refrigerated units used for transporting temperature-sensitive items like food, pharmaceuticals, and chemicals. The demand for dry containers is driven by general trade, while the reefer market serves specific industries with stringent temperature control requirements, reflecting varying logistical needs across sectors.</p></p>
