<p><ul><li>"Frame Filter Presses"</li><li>"Sheet Filter Presses"</li><li>"Chamber Filter Presses"</li><li>"Membrane Filter Presses"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Sidebar filter presses are categorized into four main types based on design and functionality. Frame filter presses consist of a system of frames that hold filter cloths, ideal for large solids. Sheet filter presses utilize flat plates for efficient filtration and are compact in size. Chamber filter presses have recesses to accommodate more solids, enhancing throughput. Membrane filter presses incorporate flexible membranes for improved washing and cake discharge, enabling higher filtration efficiency and better solid separation. Each type serves distinct industrial needs.</p></p>
