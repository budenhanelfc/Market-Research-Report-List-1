<p><p>The silicone elastomer market comprises several types, including Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR), High Consistency Rubber (HCR), Fluorosilicone Rubber, and others. LSR is versatile and commonly used in injection molding, ideal for complex shapes. HCR is thicker and suitable for applications requiring durability and high-temperature resistance. Fluorosilicone Rubber offers superior chemical and oil resistance, making it ideal for automotive and aerospace applications. The 'others' category includes specialized formulations catering to niche markets, enhancing the range of applications for silicone elastomers.</p></p>
