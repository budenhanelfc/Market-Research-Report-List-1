<p><strong>The Temperature Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Contact Type Temperature Sensor</li><li>Non-contact Type Temperature Sensor</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The temperature sensor market is divided into two main types: contact and non-contact temperature sensors. Contact type sensors directly measure temperature by physically touching the object, offering high accuracy and stability, commonly seen in industrial applications. In contrast, non-contact temperature sensors gauge thermal radiation emitted from an object, allowing for temperature measurement from a distance, ideal for moving objects or hazardous environments. Both types cater to a wide range of industries, including manufacturing, healthcare, and automation.</p></p>
