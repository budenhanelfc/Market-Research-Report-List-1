<p><p>The Transcranial Doppler (TCD) market comprises two main types: Implantable Transcranial Doppler and Functional Transcranial Doppler (fTCD). Implantable TCD devices are designed to remain in the body, allowing continuous monitoring of cerebral blood flow over extended periods. In contrast, functional TCD (fTCD) is a non-invasive method that measures blood flow velocity in real-time during specific tasks, helping assess brain function and hemodynamics. Both types play crucial roles in diagnosing and managing various neurological conditions.</p></p>
