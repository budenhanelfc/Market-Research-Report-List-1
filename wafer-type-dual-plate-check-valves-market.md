<p><ul><li>Flanged End</li><li>Threaded End</li><li>Welding End</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Wafer type dual plate check valves are classified based on their end connection types: flanged end, threaded end, and welding end. Flanged end valves feature raised, flat, or RF flanges for easy installation to pipe systems, offering robust sealing. Threaded end valves are designed for direct attachment to threaded pipes, providing a compact solution for smaller systems. Welding end valves require welding to connect with pipes, ensuring a permanent, leak-proof joint, ideal for high-pressure applications in various industries.</p></p>
